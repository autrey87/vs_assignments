Left to Right: Vladimir Putin, Donald Trump, Xi Jinping. Image source: Wall Street Journal

There are times that we all find ourselvves procrastinating for no good reason. Then there are times when the whole world holds it's collective breath.. The reason for ignoring my important school work last night was certainly the latter. Admittedly, I did it to myself when I opened my oh-so-addictive, portable Twitter Machine, but feelings of shame melted away when I was immediately greeted with the trending hashtags and topics WWIII, Trump Kills Iranian General, and China and Russia filled my feed.
